The Indigenous Collection

The Indigenous collection showcases incredible artworks that reflect living cultural traditions. This collection has objects that link to the Tent Embassy and the ongoing fight for land rights.

🐦 Ibi the Ibis

"The fish trap is extraordinary — woven with knowledge passed down through generations. These aren't just objects. They are living culture. And this collection is connected to some of the most important moments in Australian history."
