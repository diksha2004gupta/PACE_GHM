Welcome to the Gale History Museum

Acknowledgement of Country

We acknowledge the Traditional Custodians of the land on which Macquarie University stands – the Wallumattagal Clan of the Dharug Nation – whose cultures and customs have nurtured, and continue to nurture, this land since time immemorial.

We pay our respects to Elders past and present.

Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander visitors are advised that the museum contains images of, and artefacts that belonged to, deceased persons.

Opened in 2021 as part of Macquarie University’s Arts Precinct, the museum’s origins date back to 1973.

Two earlier museums—the Museum of Ancient Cultures and the Australian History Museum— merged in 2020 before being renamed in 2025 to honour Drs Janet and Bill Gale.
