A story from WW1

WW1 Story

This WWI belt belonged to Hedley Hilton Smith and bears badges commemorating fallen comrades. This object tells a story of loss and remembrance.

🐦 Ibi the Ibis

"A belt might seem like a small thing, but look at those badges. Each one is a name. A face. A friend who didn't come home. Objects like this carry grief in ways words sometimes can't."
