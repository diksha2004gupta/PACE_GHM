The Ancient Amphorae

Ancient Amphorae

Warning!
Please do not touch the amphorae! They are very old and delicate and the natural oils from our skin can damage them.

Amphorae were the essential storage and transport vessels of the ancient world, used to carry wine, olive oil, and grain across the Mediterranean.

🐦 Ibi the Ibis

"These were the shipping containers of the ancient world — used to transport wine, oil, and grain across the Mediterranean. Please don't touch them though. They've already survived two thousand years; let's not push our luck."
