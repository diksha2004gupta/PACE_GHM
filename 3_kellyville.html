Everyday Life in Kellyville

These objects were uncovered during the excavation of the White Hart Inn in Kellyville as part of the Sydney Metro Northwest expansion in 2016.

Bottles, pipes, toys, and ceramics offer a glimpse into 19th-century travel and social life.

These everyday items help us understand how people lived, travelled, and connected.
