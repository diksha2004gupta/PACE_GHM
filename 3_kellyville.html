Everyday Life in Kellyville

Kellyville Excavation

This exhibit showcases artifacts from the Kellyville excavation, revealing insights into the daily lives of 19th-century travelers and residents.

These objects were uncovered during the excavation of the White Hart Inn in Kellyville as part of the Sydney Metro Northwest expansion in 2016.

Bottles, pipes, toys, and ceramics offer a glimpse into 19th-century travel and social life.

These everyday items help us understand how people lived, travelled, and connected.

🐦 Ibi the Ibis

"Bottles, pipes, toys, ceramics — the stuff people left behind tells you everything. I once found a chip in a bin that told me more than most textbooks. History is everywhere, if you look closely enough."
